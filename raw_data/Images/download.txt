
Get Training/Testing/Validation Images from http://www.visualqa.org/download.html

Links:
	1. http://msvocds.blob.core.windows.net/coco2014/train2014.zip
	2. http://msvocds.blob.core.windows.net/coco2014/val2014.zip
	3. http://msvocds.blob.core.windows.net/coco2015/test2015.zip

Extract jpg images in respective folders.
For example *.jpg files for training set should be as:

train2014/
	COCO....jpg
	COCO....jpg
	..
	..

You can take subset of images in put in -sub directories for simple test.
